How Celebrity Buzz Index Works

Celebrity Buzz Index is the ultimate fantasy celebrity game. Build your dream team of up to 10 celebrities using your £50M budget. Each celebrity has a price based on their current media buzz - A-list stars like Taylor Swift and Beyoncé cost more, while rising stars offer great value.

Your team earns points whenever your celebrities appear in the news. The more headlines they make, the more points you score! Transfer windows open every Saturday, giving you 24 hours to make up to 3 swaps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Celebrity Buzz Index free to play?

Yes! Celebrity Buzz Index is completely free. Create your account, build your team, and compete on the leaderboard without paying anything.

How are celebrity prices calculated?

Prices are based on media buzz scores derived from news coverage, social media presence, and recent headlines. Prices update weekly based on real-world activity.

What is the Brown Bread Watch?

The Brown Bread Watch shows elderly celebrities. If one of your team members passes away, you receive a +100 point bonus - a strategic but morbid feature!
