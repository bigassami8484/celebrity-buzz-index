    -   Support Google social login and passwordless magic link email login.
-   **Features**:

    -   A Hot Celebs This Week banner with real photos, dynamically populated by celebrities currently in the news.
    -   Private Friends Leagues for users to create/join.
    -   A Brown Bread Watch for elderly celebrities with special premium pricing.
    -   Price history tracking for each celebrity.
-   **UI/UX**: The application must be mobile-friendly, with a clean and uncluttered interface. The main title's size is a key priority for the user.
